Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on CareerConnect?
Creating an account is simple! Click the "Register" button in the top right corner of the page. You'll need to provide your email address, create a password, and fill in some basic information about your professional background. Once you've verified your email, you're all set to explore jobs and companies!
Is it free to use CareerConnect?
Yes, basic access to CareerConnect is completely free for job seekers! You can create a profile, search for jobs, and apply to positions at no cost. We also offer Premium membership options with additional features like advanced search filters, application insights, and priority support.
How can companies post job listings?
Employers can create a company account and access our job posting platform from the "For Employers" section. We offer various recruitment packages to suit businesses of all sizes, from startups to enterprise companies. For more information, please contact our sales team at partners@careerconnect.com.
I forgot my password. How can I reset it?
If you've forgotten your password, click "Login" at the top of the page, then select "Forgot password?" below the login form. Enter your email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password. For security reasons, the reset link expires after 24 hours.
How can I get technical support?
For technical issues or account-related questions, you can contact our support team at support@careerconnect.com or call +1 (800) 555-0123 during business hours. Premium members also have access to 24/7 live chat support directly through the platform.
